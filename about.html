    • Here is my list below, ranked from best to worst, in my opinion:

    1. Talk to Me (2023)

      • Talk to Me is a supernatural horror film about a group of friends who experiment with a mysterious, embalmed hand that allows them to communicate with spirits. Things take a drastic turn, however, as they start to reveal the terrifying consequences that come with talking to the dead.

    2. Evil Dead Rise (2023)

      • Evil Dead Rise is a horror film that follows two sisters and their children who must survive when demonics forces are awakened in their apartment building.

    3. Us (2019)

      • Us is a psychological horror film that follows a family who encounters terrifying dopplegangers of themselves while vacationing at a beach house. As the night unfolds, they must fight to survive to see who will get to stay in the world above.

    4. Orphan (2009)

      • Orphan is a psychological horror thriller about a couple who adopts a 9-year-old girl named Esther after losing their own child. However, as time goes on, they start to discover her sinister past, leading to shocking relevations about her true identity.

    5. Veronica (2017)

      • Veronica is a Spanish horror film based on true events. It follows a girl named Veronica who, after using a Ouija board to contact her deceased father, becomes plagued by terrifying supernatural occurences, where she must learn to fight and protect her family from her mistake.

    6. The Descent (2005)

      • The Descent is a horror film about a group of women who go on a caving expedition in a remote area. As they go further into the cave, they encounter a terrifying species of underground creatures who gave adapted to living down there, forcing them to fight for their own survival.

    7. The Night House (2020)

      • The Night House is a psychological horror film about a widow named Beth, who begins to uncover unsettling truths about her late husband's life as she explores their lakeside home.

    8. You're Next (2011)

      • You're Next is home-invasion horror film about a family reunion that turns deadly when a group of masked assailants attacks their home, turning their reunion into a sudden fight for survival between everyone.

    9. Hush (2016)

      • Hush is a suspenseful horror-thriller about a deaf writer named Maddie, who is living by herself in the woods when she is suddenly attacked by a masked killer. She must survive by using her wits and resourcefulness despite her physical limitations.

    10. Triangle (2009)

      • Triangle is a psycholgical thriller-horror about a group of friends who become stranded on an abandoned ship after a storm. As they try to escape, they realize they're caught in a loop, with terrifying and deadly consequences around every corner.
